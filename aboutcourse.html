› About Course

TastyPython is originally designed for research intrested students looking for a good introduction to programming. However it is also well suitable for young teens, especially the 12-16 year olds. You'll not learn it like a school-subject, because programming is not a school-subject. A project is assigned every week and towards the end of the course you'll be given a independent project of your own that will include everything that you learnt unitl now. The project can be few months long, and it is meant to be a testimonial for skill you learnt. A certificate is awarded on finishing the project and you'll have plenty of work to do after that. So hop along and join us for an exciting Python course.
